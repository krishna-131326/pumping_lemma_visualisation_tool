What is the Pumping Lemma?

01

The Lemma

For every regular language L, there exists a pumping length p such that any string s ∈ L with |s| ≥ p can be split as s = xyz satisfying three conditions.

02

Three Conditions

  • |y| ≥ 1 (y is non-empty)
  • |xy| ≤ p (stays within pump length)
  • ∀i ≥ 0 : xyiz ∈ L (all pumpings stay in L)
03

Proof Strategy

To prove L is not regular: assume it is, apply the lemma, then find a string where at least one pumped version xyiz ∉ L — a contradiction!
